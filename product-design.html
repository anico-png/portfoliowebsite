Product Design

Why redesign a stove, you might ask? Through this project, I uncovered key issues that users face—problems they don’t always voice but definitely feel.

While I chose not to go fully digital, since people love the tactile experience of cooking, I saw room for improvement. I’ve added some futuristic touches to enhance the cooking process, making it more efficient, stress-free, and enjoyable, while still keeping that hands-on connection people love!

Futuristic Stove

SASA is a new and redesigned ideation of a vintage sewing machine inspired by the form of a mushroom (Reishi) to capture the endless beauty of the organic curves.


It is designed to rejuvenate the lively spirit of the user. SASA symbolises new beginnings, growth, and the potential forpositive change.
